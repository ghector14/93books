About 93books

Our Mission

93books exists to make knowledge accessible. We believe that everyone deserves access to the wisdom contained in great books, but not everyone has the time to read hundreds of pages. Our detailed summaries capture the essential concepts, key insights, and actionable takeaways from each book, giving you the knowledge you need in a fraction of the time.

The Story Behind 93books

93books was created by Hector, a track and cross country coach with a deep love for running and reading. As a competitive runner, he understands the discipline of incremental progress—how small, consistent efforts compound into extraordinary results. That same philosophy applies to reading and learning.

Reading, for him, is like that iconic scene from The Matrix where Neo downloads martial arts directly into his brain. "I know Kung Fu." That's the power of books, you can download decades of someone's experience, research, and wisdom in just hours. 93books exists to make that download even faster, distilling complex ideas into clear, actionable summaries so you can absorb knowledge at the speed of thought.

The name "93" represents the pursuit of excellence and the belief that consistent effort leads to extraordinary results, much like the incremental progress made through reading and learning.

How We Summarize

Each book summary on 93books is carefully crafted to capture the author's core message while preserving the context and nuance that makes their work valuable. Our approach includes:

  • Chapter by chapter breakdowns that maintain the book's structure and flow
  • Key concepts highlighted for quick reference and understanding
  • Actionable insights you can apply immediately to your life
  • Context preservation so you understand not just what, but why
  • Paragraph format that reads naturally, not just bullet points

What We Read

Our collection focuses on books that offer genuine value and transformative insights across multiple categories:

  • Self-Development - Building better habits, mindset, and personal growth
  • Psychology - Understanding human behavior and the mind
  • Science - Exploring the universe, physics, and our place in it
  • Business & Success - Strategies for achievement and influence
  • Philosophy & Meaning - Finding purpose and living with intention
